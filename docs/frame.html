Assembly Instructions¶

Required Tools¶

  • Phillips screwdriver
  • Flathead screwdriver
  • Allen wrench (included with shaft coupler)

Instructions¶

  • (4) M3 threaded inserts
  • (8) Bumpers

  • (2) Springs
  • (1) Grommet
  • (1) Motor
  1. Assemble end support
  • (1) Flanged ball bearing
  • (1) Grommet
  1. Attach lead screw to motor
  • (1) Cradle Assembly
  • (1) Lead screw
  • (1) Coupler

  • (1) End support assembly

  • (1) Base assembly

  • (1) End support assembly

  • (4) M4 x 16mm screws
