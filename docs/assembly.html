Assembly Instructions¶

Required Tools¶

  • Phillips screwdriver
  • Allen wrench (included with shaft coupler)

Instructions¶

  1. Mount motor
  1. Add coupler
  1. Assemble syringe carriage
  1. Assemble stage
  1. Mount syringe carriage and stage
  1. Assemble end support
  1. Attach rubber bumper to base
  1. Mount everything to the base
  1. Attach connector to wires, mount controller to end support, and attach magnet to linear bearing
  1. Attach syringe
  1. Done!
