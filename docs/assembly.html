Assembly Instructions¶

Mechanical¶

Required Tools¶

  • Phillips head screwdriver
  • allen wrench

Instructions¶

  1. Press fit threaded inserts into acrylic
  2. Attach the stepper motor to acrylic piece “A” using (4) M4 x 10 mm screws
  3. Use 5/64” allen wrench to secure shaft coupler to
  4. Use 3mm allen wrench to shaft support to the shaft
